ABOUT


Looking for Your Next Adventure?

  Arcadia is a thrilling and immersive web application designed for gamers and adventure seekers alike. Whether you're looking to relive old gaming memories or discover new titles, Arcadia is the perfect platform for you.

How Does It Work?

  Arcadia's game search engine allows you to easily find your favorite games based on parameters such as platform, genre, year, and rating. Additionally, the advanced game recommender analyzes your gaming preferences to suggest new titles that match your interests. Simply add games to your profile to keep track of your progress and receive personalized recommendations.

What Can You Expect?

  With a homepage filled with the latest releases, highest rated games, and a comprehensive list of titles sorted by platform, genre, and rating, you can easily find exciting new games to play. Click on a game card to access a detailed summary that provides all the information you need to decide whether it's worth playing.

How Can You Personalize Your Experience?

  Create a personalized profile on Arcadia that tracks your gaming progress and saves your preferences. Connect with other gamers and explore the social side of gaming by adding friends to your account. In the future, enjoy even more personalized experiences with email verification/recovery options and more specific search parameters.
